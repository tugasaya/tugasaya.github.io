Jawaban:

3%

Penjelasan:

BANK A

a x 5/100 = 2.275.000

5a = 227.500.000

a = 227.500.000/5

a = 45.500.000 (total sum of money save at bank a)

BANK B

b x 7/100 = 4.900.000

7b = 490.000.000

b = 490.000.000/7

b = 70.000.000 (total sum of money save at bank b)

BANK C

total sum of money save at bank c = 150.000.000 - (a+b)

c = 150.000.000 - (45.500.000 + 70.000.000)
c = 150.000.000 - 115.500.000

c = 34.500.000

the interest of bank c is = 35.535.000 - 34.500.000 = 1.035.000

c x α/100 = 1.035.000

34.500.000 x α/100 = 1.035.000

34.500.000α = 103.500.000

α = 103.500.000/34.500.000

α = 3

so, the interest rate of bank C is 3%

8. Andrew deposited some money in the bank at the beginning of the year. At the end of one year, he earned an interest and had $3045 in the bank. This was 101.5% of the amount of money Andrew deposited at the beginning of the year. a) How much did Andrew deposit in the bank at the beginning of the year? b) What was the amount of interest Andrew earned?​


Jawab:

a) Andrew deposited $3000 in the bank at the beginning of the year.

b) The amount of interest he earned was $45. The interest rate in that bank is 1.5% per year.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

The amount of Andrew's money in the bank at the end of one year is 101.5% of the amount of money he deposited at the beginning of the year:

$3045 = 101.5% × m

(m = the amount of money Andrew deposited at the beginning of the year)

$3045 = 101.5% × m

m = $3045 ÷ 101.5%

   = $3045 ÷ (101.5/100)

   = $3045 × 100/101.5

   = $3000

∴ Therefore, Andrew deposited $3000 in the bank at the beginning of the year.

The amount of interest he earned:

$3045 – $3000 = $45

Because this is the amount of interest in one year, we can say that the interest rate in that bank is 1.5% per year.
