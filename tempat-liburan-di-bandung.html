Contoh teks Recount Liburan di Bandung adalah:

Holiday in Bandung

When I was 3rd grade of Junior High School, my friends and I went to Ciwidey, Bandung to spent our holiday after the National Exams. We stayed at homestay for two days and visited many places there. We went there by car at 6.00 a.m. It took about 3 hours from Jakarta to Bandung.

The traffic to Bandung was smooth. At 9 a.m we already arrived at Ciwidey area. The first place we want to visit is Kawah Putih. We just enjoy the scenery, looking at villas or some cute homestays along the way to Kawah Putih, when we realized here was a sign "Welcome to Kawah Putih". Because the smell of sulfur is strong from the lake, we decided to bought masks. But at last, we didn’t use it at all. The scenery here was so beautiful. Many visitors took selfie picture, including us of course. We took so much photo. We met so many other tourists there. They were not only domestic but also foreign tourists. Two hours later, we decided to left Kawah Putih. Around 1 p.m, we had lunch.

Then we went to Situ Patenggang. It is a lake that surounded by tea plantation. The location is close from Kawah Putih. It was cool there. The breeze blew smoothly. We took a boat to bring us explore the lake. The lake is small and has an island in the middle of it. Then, we took a photo, again. We didn't take a long time there, because we wanted to take a rest at homestay. Around 5 p.m we checked in to the homestay. We had a dinner and several hours later, we went to bed.

In the morning, we woke up around 7 a.m and we were ready to go to Strawberry plantation at Desa Alam Endah. The air here was so pure and all we could see is strawberry everywhere.

In the evening, we had to check out from the homestay. We went back to Jakarta bringing so many amazing memories of Bandung.

19. Tolong buat kan recount text 3 paragraf bahasa inggris tentang liburan ke bandung dengan keluarga saat liburan sekolah​


During my recent school holiday, my family and I took a trip to Bandung. It was a much-awaited break from the daily routine and we were all excited to explore the city and its surroundings.

We spent most of our time visiting the various tourist attractions in the city, such as Tangkuban Perahu, Sari Ater Hot Spring, and Ciwidey Valley. We also visited several famous shopping centers, such as Paris Van Java and Trans Studio Mall, where we indulged in some retail therapy. The food in Bandung was delicious, and we made sure to try all the local delicacies, such as sate maranggi and mie kocok.

Overall, our trip to Bandung was a fantastic experience. It was great to spend quality time with my family and make new memories. We also learned a lot about the local culture and history, and I would highly recommend this destination to anyone looking for a fun and relaxing holiday.
