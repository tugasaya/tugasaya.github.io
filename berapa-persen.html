sisi = a

Luas = a²

Pertambahan panjang sisi

s = a + 40% a = 1,4a

Luas = s² = (1,4a)² = 1,96a²

Persentase pertambahan luas

= selisih luas/luas awal × 100%

= (1,96a² - a²)/a² × 100%

= 0,96 × 100%
