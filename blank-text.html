We went by just Brahmana and Syiwa sanctuaries, on the grounds that Wisnu sanctuary is being remodeled. On Saturday morning we went to Yogya Kraton. We spent around two hours there. We were fortunate on the grounds that we were driven by a brilliant and amicable aide. At that point we proceeded with our adventure to Borobudur. We touched base there at four p.m. At 6 p.m. we heard the declaration that Borobudur entryway would be closed. In the evening we left Jogjakarta by bus.

5. fill the blank spaces in the following text with the subordinating conjunction provided in the box


Jawaban:

1. Though

2. Although

3. Before

4. Before

5. Because

6. After

7. Wherever

8. So that

9. So that

10. As long as

Penjelasan:

• Though = meskipun; though biasanya digunakan pada kalimat yang lebih santai dan tidak formal.

• Although = meskipun; although banyak di gunakan untuk menghubungkan kalimat yang bersifat formal atau resmi.

• Before = sebelum; rumus: Before + Earlier event (main clause) + later event (dependent clause)

• After = sesudah; rumus: After + Later event (main clause) + earlier event (dependent clause)

• Because = karena

• Wherever = dimanapun; berasal dari kata "where" yang memiliki arti "dimana".

• So that = jadi; So that digunakan untuk menunjukkan tujuan dari sesuatu (to show the purpose of something).

• As long as = selama; as long as harus diikuti oleh present tense dimana ia menunjukan kejadian yang akan datang.
