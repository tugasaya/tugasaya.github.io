How to make:

-First, make chicken noodle broth soup first, by boiling the boiled water again with chicken meat added with pepper, salt, and chicken bones to taste.

-Both, make chicken topping.

Boiled chicken is then chopped into small pieces or finely chopped.

Blend the seasoning consisting of garlic, onion, coriander, turmeric, candlenut, and ginger.

When boiling the noodles, prepare a bowl.

Then add 1 tablespoon of chicken oil and 1 teaspoon of pepper.

Put the cooked noodles in the bowl.

Then stir the noodles until they are completely mixed with chicken oil.

Add chicken topping and chicken soup.

Chicken noodles are ready to be served.

11. Cara membuat resep mie ayam pake bahasa inggris


⚫Ingredients

½ a bunch of fresh coriander

3 cloves of garlic

5 cm piece of ginger

1 bunch of spring onions

1 fresh red chilli

1 lime

2 carrots

1 head of broccoli

2 skinless higher-welfare chicken breasts

250 g medium free-range egg noodles

50 g unsalted cashew nuts

vegetable oil

freshly ground black pepper

1-2 tablespoons low-salt soy sauce

1 tablespoon fish sauce

⚫Method

1.Pick the coriander leaves and finely slice the stalks, then keep aside for later. Peel and finely slice the garlic, then peel and matchstick the ginger.

2. Trim and finely slice the spring onions, deseed and finely slice the chilli and cut the lime into wedges. Trim, peel and thinly slice the carrots at an angle.

3.Cut the broccoli into small florets, then finely shred the stalk, discarding any gnarly bits. Cut the chicken into 1cm strips.

4.Cook the noodles according to packet instructions, then drain and refresh under cold water. Drain again, toss in a little oil and put to one side. Lightly toast the cashew nuts in a non-stick frying pan until golden, then tip onto a plate to cool.

5.Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large frying pan or wok. Season the chicken with a pinch of pepper, then add to the pan and stir-fry for 2 to 3 minutes, or until golden. Add the coriander stalks, garlic and ginger and cook for a further minute.

6.Add the spring onions, carrots and broccoli and stir-fry for a further 2 minutes, then add the cooked noodles. Keep stir-frying until the noodles are warm and the chicken is cooked through. Stir through the soy and fish sauces, then remove from the heat.

7.Divide between bowls, sprinkle over the nuts, sliced chilli and the reserved coriander leaves, then serve with the lime wedges for squeezing over.

analisis swot mie ayam, yaitu :

strength (kekuatan) :

a. memiliki harga yang terjangkau

b. memiliki banyak peminat

c. menggunakan bahan2 yang mudah ditemukan dipasaran.

weaknes (kelemahan) :

a. banyak pesaing

b. harga sewa tempat yang tinggi

c. jumlah konsumen tidak menentu

opportunities (peluang) :

a. memberikan peluang kerja bagi org lain

b. memajukan makanan lokal

c. menyajikannya dengan kreatif dan inovatif.

threats (ancaman) :

a. kenaikan harga bahan baku yang tidak menentu

b. ketersediaan bahan baku yang tidak menentu

c. berkembangnya makanan modern lainnya.

26. membuat teks deskripsi tentang mie ayam dalam b.inggris​


Chicken Noodle

Chicken noodle is one of the favorite foods of the Indonesian people. This food is very easy to find on the side of the road at an affordable price. Chicken noodle actually come from China.

These noodles are usually served hot. In a bowl of chicken noodles, it usually consists of noodles made from wheat, mustard greens, green onions, broth and also pieces of chicken meat on top.

Indonesian people really like chicken noodles because the price is affordable and many sell it, also because chicken noodles have many variations of toppings such as dumplings, meatballs, mushrooms, claws and many more. Not only that, chicken noodles are also very suitable to eat during the rainy season like now.

Jawaban:

Mie Ayam is a popular Indonesian dish consisting of egg noodles served with chicken meat, often in a savory broth or sauce. The dish is typically garnished with green onions, celery, and a variety of condiments, such as soy sauce, chili sauce, and lime wedges. The broth is made by boiling chicken bones, meat and other ingredients, such as garlic, ginger, onions, and sometimes also contain mushrooms, tomatoes and other vegetables, which infuse it with a rich, savory flavor. The dish is usually served with a side of steamed or blanched vegetables, and is considered a comforting and satisfying meal for both locals and tourists.

how to make chicken noodle

Material:

-noodle wet or noodles

- 500 g eggs

Chicken 300 g

500 ml chicken oil

- Garlic 4 cloves

-Onion 4 pieces

-Cumbar 1 tsp

Finger turmeric

-Kiri 4 items

-Jahe 1 segment

- 2 L Chicken Stew Stew

- Pepper powder 1 tsp

- 1 teaspoon salt

- Chicken bone to taste

How to make:

-First, make chicken noodle broth soup first, by boiling the boiled water again with chicken meat added with pepper, salt, and chicken bones to taste.

-Both, make chicken topping.

Boiled chicken is then chopped into small pieces or finely chopped.

Blend the seasoning consisting of garlic, onion, coriander, turmeric, candlenut, and ginger.

Saute the chicken with mashed spices.

Add salt and flavoring to taste.

After the chicken noodle soup and chicken topping are ready, boil the prepared noodles.

When boiling the noodles, prepare a bowl.

Then add 1 tablespoon of chicken oil and 1 teaspoon of pepper.

Put the cooked noodles in the bowl.

Then stir the noodles until they are completely mixed with chicken oil.

Add chicken topping and chicken soup.

Chicken noodles are ready to be served.

Bahan:

-mie basah atau mie

- 500 g telur

Ayam 300 g

-500 ml minyak ayam

- Bawang putih 4 siung

-Bawang 4 buah

-Cumbar 1 sdt

Kunyit jari

-Kiri 4 item

-Jahe 1 segmen

- 2 L Rebusan Ayam

- Bubuk lada 1 sdt

- 1 sendok teh garam

- Tulang ayam secukupnya

Bagaimana membuat:

-Pertama-tama, buat sup kaldu mie ayam terlebih dahulu, dengan merebus air matang lagi dengan daging ayam ditambah lada, garam, dan tulang ayam secukupnya.

-Kedua, buat topping ayam.

Ayam rebus ini kemudian dipotong-potong kecil atau dicincang halus.

Blender bumbu yang terdiri dari bawang putih, bawang merah, ketumbar, kunyit, kemiri, dan jahe.

Tumis ayam dengan bumbu halus.

Tambahkan garam dan bumbu secukupnya.

Setelah sup mie ayam dan topping ayam siap, rebus mie yang sudah disiapkan.

Saat merebus mie, siapkan mangkuk.

Kemudian tambahkan 1 sendok makan minyak ayam dan 1 sendok teh lada.

Masukkan mie yang sudah dimasak ke dalam mangkuk.

Kemudian aduk mie sampai benar-benar tercampur dengan minyak ayam.

Tambahkan topping ayam dan sup ayam.

Mie ayam siap disajikan
