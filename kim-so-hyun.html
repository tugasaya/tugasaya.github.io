17. change the adjective/adverb in the brackets into degree comparison form!1.my dictionary is (thin) than hasan's2.in my opinion, the (handsome) Korean actor ia Kim Soo Hyun.​


Jawaban:

Your Questions:

My dictionary is (thin) than Hasan's. In my opinion, the (handsome) Korean actor is Kim Soo Hyun.

Answer:

My dictionary is thinner than Hasan's. In my opinion, the most handsome Korean actor is Kim Soo Hyun.

Type of sentences: Comparative and Superlative degrees.

Comparative degree.

1 kali pengucapannya.

Subject+to be(is,am,are)+adjective+ier/er/r+than+object.

2 kali atau lebih pengucapannya.

Subject+to be(is,am,are)+more+adjective+than+object.

Superlative degree.

1 kali pengucapannya.

Subject+to be(is,am,are)+the+adjective+iest/est/st+object.

2 kali atau lebih pengucapannya.

Subject+to be(is,am,are)+the most+adjective+object.

Example:

She is taller than me. She is the tallest girl in this classroom. My mother is more beautiful than me. My mother is the most beautiful girl in this area.

Kelas: SMP

Kata Kunci: Comparison degrees
