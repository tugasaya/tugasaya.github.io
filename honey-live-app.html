Honey bear is the length of its body on its back, height 1.40 m 70 cm with a weight ranging from 50-65 kg. Because of its life in the trees then the soles of the feet not hairy bears this so that he can move at speeds up to 48 kilometers per hour and has a very strong power. This is a honey bear mammals, honey bear Fur tends to be short, shiny and black in General, under the fur around his neck there is a unique orange-colored sign that is believed to depict Sunrise blue or brown eyes, besides his nose is relatively wide but not too the muzzle.
     Honey bear is omnivorous animals that eat up anything in the forest. They eat a variety of fruits and a tropical rainforest plants, including the plant's buds are also a type of Palm. They also eat insects, honey, birds, and other small animals. Honey bear has been considered an easy beast in attack and threatened his survival. This is caused by the pengerusakan the habitat sustained. Greatest threat to honey bear is indeed the more habitat loss in the form of a tropical rain forest, including forest fragmentation and degradation of the forest caused by human behavior in the form of illegal wild forest and deforestation for the purposes of rubber plantations, palm oil and coffee.
