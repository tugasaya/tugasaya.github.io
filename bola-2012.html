7. Hasil dari 45+4^2014-4^2012/3+4^2012 adalah


[tex] \frac{45+4^{2014}-4^{2012}}{3+4^{2012}} = 15 \: \:. \\ \\ [/tex]

Pembahasan

Sifat-sifat bilangan berpangkat

[tex]\boxed{{a}^{ \frac{b}{c} } = \sqrt[c]{ {a}^{b} } } \\ \\ \boxed{{a}^{ {b}^{c} } = {a}^{ ({b}^{c}) }} \\ \\ \boxed{{a}^{b} \cdot {a}^{c} = {a}^{b + c}} \\ \\ \boxed{\frac{ {a}^{b} }{ {a}^{c} } = {a}^{b - c}} \\ \\ \boxed{{e}^{ ln(x) } = x} \\ \\ [/tex]

Diketahui :

[tex] \frac{45+4^{2014}-4^{2012}}{3+4^{2012}} \\ \\ [/tex]

Ditanya :

[tex] \text{Hasil dari } \: \: \frac{45+4^{2014}-4^{2012}}{3+4^{2012}} \: \:. \\ \\ [/tex]

Jawab :

[tex] \text{Gunakan sifat : } \: a^{b+c} = a^b \cdot a^c \:. \\ [/tex]

[tex] \begin{aligned} \frac{45+4^{2014}-4^{2012}}{3+4^{2012}} & \: = \frac{45+4^{2012+2}-4^{2012}}{3+4^{2012}} \\ \\ \: & = \frac{45+4^2 \cdot 4^{2012}-4^{2012}}{3+4^{2012}} \\ \\ \: & = \frac{45+4^{2012}\cdot (4^2-1)}{3+4^{2012}} \\ \\ \: & = \frac{45+15 \cdot 4^{2012}}{3+4^{2012}} \\ \\ \: & = \frac{15\left(3+4^{2012}\right)}{3+4^{2012}} \\ \\ \: & = \frac{15\left(\cancel{3+4^{2012}}\right)}{\cancel{3+4^{2012}}} \\ \\ \: & = 15 \\ \\ \end{aligned} [/tex]

Kesimpulan :

[tex] \text{Hasil } \: \: \frac{45+4^{2014}-4^{2012}}{3+4^{2012}} = 15 \: \:. \\ \\ [/tex]

= (45 + 4(2.012 + 2) - 4^(2.012)) / (3 + 4^(2.012))
= (45 + 4^(2.012) x 4^2 - 4^(2.012)) / (3 + 4^(2.012))
= (45 + 16 . 4^(2.012) - 4^(2.012)) / (3 + 4^(2.012))
= (45 + 15 . 4^(2.012)) / (3 + 4^(2.012))
= 15 (3 + 4^(2.012)) / (3 + 4^(2.012))
= 15
