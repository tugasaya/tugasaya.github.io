Writing Recount Text

Yesterday I finally watched my favorite movie of all time, Avengers: Endgame with the Marvel lovers community in my city. We already bought the ticket the week before just in case to avoid sold out tickets.

We gathered in premiere XXI in Plaza Senayan at 11 a.m. with the rest of community members. Some of them wore marvel t-shirt just like me. I wore my favorite Black Widow t-shirt since she is my favorite character in the movie.

Before the movie started, we bought some snacks too like popcorn and soda. The studio was full at that day, but fortunately we got the best seat in the middle.

Overall, I enjoyed the movie so much. The community was really supportive as well about it. I didn't regret join the community because I met many new friends who shares the same passion for marvel.

15. Dua buah muatan yang memiliki jarak R memiliki gaya Coulomb sebesar F. Jika jarak kedua muatan diubah menjadi 2R, gaya Coulomb antara kedua muatan tersebut menjadi… https://mamby.com/post/hq-online-avengers-endgame-2019-online-full-english-movie-free-hdq


Adapun persamaan pada hukum coulomb yaitu sebagai berikut :

F = k q1.q2/r²

keterangan :

F = gaya coulomb (satuan N)

k = konstanta pembanding (besarnya 9 x 10'9 Nm²/C²)

q1 = muatan 1 (satuan coulomb)

q2 = muatan 2 (satuan coulomb)

r = jarak antar muatan (satuan m)

contoh

Selanjutnya yang ditanyakan pada soal no 1 adalah F₂ jika jaraknya menjadi 2 kali semula (r₂ = 2r₁) dan pada soal no 2 F₂ jika muatannya menjadi 2Q.

Penyelesaian soal no 1

F1/F2 = kq1q2/r1² / kq1q2/r2²

F1/F2 = kq1q2/r1² x r2²/kq1q2 (kq1q2 di coret)

F1/F2 = r2²/r1²

F1/F2 = 2r1²/r1²

F1/F2 = 4r1²/r1² (r1² dicoret)

F1/F2 = 4

F2 = 1/4 F1

Penyelesaian soal no 2

F1/F2 = kq1q2/r1² / kq1q2/r2²

F1/F2 = kq1q2/r1² / kq1q2/r2²

(k, r1² dan r2² di coret)

F1/F2 = q1q2/q1q2

F1/F2 = q1q2/2q12q2

F1/F2 = q1q2/4q1q2

F1/F2 = 1/4 (q1q2 dicoret)

4F1 = 1F2

F2 = 4 F1

anggota avengers

= 1.000 : 375
