3. The distance between City X and City Y is 280 km. The distance between City Y and City Z is 238 km. Find the average speed of the bullet train for the whole journey from City X to City Z in km/h. kak tolong jawab aku hari ini di kumpul


DISTANCE

CITY X TO CITY Y = 280 KM

CITY Y TO CITY Z = 238 KM

CITY X TO CITY Z = 280 + 238 = 518 KM

TIME

CITY X TO CITY Y = 54 MIN

CITY Y TO CITY Z = 51 MIN

CITY X TO CITY Z = 54 + 51 = 105 MIN = 1 HR 45 MIN (1,75 HR)

AVG. SPEED

518 ÷ 1,75 = 296 KM / H

26. The distance of City A to City B is 60 km, the distance of two city is on a map is 3 cm, the scale of two city is … *tolong nanti ku follow sisa 2 menit​


Jawab:

1 : 2.000.000

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Known:-Real distance=60 km

            -Map distance=3 cm

Question: Scale

Answer:

*60 km = ....cm

            = ( 60 × 100.000 ) × 1 cm

            = 6.000.000 cm

*Scale = Map distance : Real distance

           = 3 cm : 6.000.000 cm ( scratch the 'cm' )

           = 1 : 2.000.000

So, the scale of the map is 1 : 2.000.000
