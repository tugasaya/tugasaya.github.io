12. yg temasuk dalam kalimat literally or figuratively


Literal language: the words mean exactly what is said.
Figurative language: the words aren't literally true.

Examples of Literal language:
• The sun is bright.
• She smiled at me.
• She is beautiful.
• Her hair is smooth.
• She runs fast.

Examples of the figurative language:
• The sun smiled so bright.
• She is like a flower.
• Her hair was silk.
• She is as fast as the wind.

28. Penggunaan literally itu buat gimana aja sih? tolong jelasin yang lengkap ya.. kasih contoh juga.. makasih sebelumnya.. ​


Jawaban:

Literally merupakan adverb (kata keterangan bahasa Inggris) yang memiliki beberapa macam penggunaan.

Penjelasan:

-Literally bermakna “in a literal way” (secara harfiah) dimana memiliki sinonim “exactly” (secara tepat).

CONTOH: She meant it as a joke, but they took it literally.

-Literally digunakan untuk memberi penekanan pada statement (pernyataan) atau description (deskripsi) yang benar adanya dan akurat meskipun mungkin terdengar mengejutkan.

CONTOH: There were literally thousands of people.

-(informal) Literally digunakan untuk memberi penekanan pada pernyataan atau deskripsi yang tidak benar atau mungkin secara harfiah.

CONTOH: They literally stole my heart by their amazing personalities.
