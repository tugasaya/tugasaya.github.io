6. what is meant by weak obligation ? mention the modal verbs used to express weak obligations !​


Jawaban:

Weak obligation is advice as the right/ correct thing to do. But it is not a must.

Weak obligation use should and ought to.

E.g.

You ought to prepare your bags the night before you go to Hawaii. (It is a good idea.)

Our teacher is unwell. We should visit her. (It is a nice thing to do.)
