5. sebanyak 100 ml larutan HCL 0,2 M. ditambahkan 400 ml air tentukan PH larutan tersebut?????? jawab secara detail dan sederhana supaya mudah dipahami. jazakallahu khairan ^-^


100 mL HCl 0,2 M + 400 mL air

pH ... ?

V1 = 100 mL
M1 = 0,2 M
V2 = 100 + 400 = 500 mL
M2 = belum diketahui

....... V1 × M1 = V2 × M2
.... 100 × 0,2 = 500 × M2
............... 20 = 500 × M2
...... 20/500 = M2
............ 0,04 = M2
.............. M2 = 0,04 M

[H^+] = a × Ma
[H^+] = 1 × 0,04
[H^+] = 0,04
[H^+] = 4 × 10^-2

pH = - log [H^+]
pH = - log 4 × 10^-2
pH = 2 - log 4

24. Diketahui f(x)=(10x-5)/(5x+4) tentukan f-¹(-1)!bantuin dong ka:'( sebelumnya jazakallahu khair​


Jawaban

[tex]\boxed{f^{-1}(x) = \frac{-4x - 5}{5x - 10} }[/tex] atau [tex]\boxed{f^{-1}(x) = -\frac{4x + 5}{5x - 10}}[/tex]

.

Langkah penyelesaian

[tex]f(x) = \frac{10x - 5}{5x + 4}[/tex]

[tex]y = \frac{10x - 5}{5x + 4}[/tex]

[tex]y(5x + 4) = 10x - 5[/tex]

[tex]5xy + 4y = 10x - 5[/tex]

[tex]5xy - 10x = -4y - 5[/tex]

[tex]x(5y - 10) = -4y - 5[/tex]

[tex]x = \frac{-4y - 5}{5y - 10}[/tex]

[tex]f^{-1}(x) = \frac{-4x - 5}{5x - 10}[/tex]
