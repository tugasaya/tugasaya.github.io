Answer: 

The inquiry above seems to be about the instruction on doing something together. Since it's about prayers for all religion. It has to be made formal and containing general hope and decency. 
Here's the example of such prayers:
- Before the school begins:
  'Let us pray. O The Almighty God, please assist us and give us blessing today so that we can learn our lessons at our best. May they be useful for our better life and future. Amen.'

- After school
  'Let us pray. O The Almighty God, thank you for your blessing today so that we can learn our lessons and gain the essence of them. May they be useful for our better life and future. Amen.'
