Soal No. 1

Nilai dari 22 + 23 + 24 adalah….

A. 28

B. 48

C. 512

Pembahasan

22 + 23 + 24

= (2×2) + (2×2×2) + (2×2×2×2)

= 4 + 8 + 16

= 28

Jawaban: A

Soal No. 2

Nilai dari 22 ⋅ 23 ⋅ 24 adalah….

A. 128

B. 256

C. 512

Pembahasan

22 ⋅ 23 ⋅ 24

= (2×2) × (2×2×2) × (2×2×2×2)

= 4×8×16

= 512

Jawaban: C

Bisa juga seperti ini, 22 ⋅ 23 ⋅ 24

= 2(2 + 3 + 4)

= 29

= 512
