5. apa perbedaan present continuous tense,past continuous tense,dan future continuous tense


Perbedaan present continuous tense, past continuous tense, dan future continuous tense adalahPresent continuous tense menunjukkan kegiatan di masa sekarang, past continuous tense menunjukkan kegiatan di masa lampau, dan future continuous tense menunjukkan kegiatan di masa mendatang (belum terjadi).Present continuous tense menggunakan is/am/are, past continuous tense menggunakan was/were, dan future continuous tense menggunakan will be.

Simak penjelasan berikut.

Pembahasan

Continuous tenses merupakan bentuk kalimat yang menyatakan kegiatan sedang berlangsung dan dimulai sebelum masa tertentu yang disebutkan dalam pembicaran. Tiga macam continuous tenses yang akan dibahas di sini, yaitu present continuous tense, past continuous tense, dan future continuous tense.

Present continuous tense menunjukkan kegiatan di masa sekarang, past continuous tense menunjukkan kegiatan di masa lampau, dan future continuous tense menunjukkan kegiatan di masa mendatang (belum terjadi). Ketiganya menjelaskan bahwa kegiatan sedang berlangsung pada masanya masing-masing.

Ketiga tenses menggunakan kata kerja pelengkap (auxiliary verb) 'be'. Present continuous tense menggunakan is/am/are, past continuous tense menggunakan was/were, dan future continuous tense menggunakan will be.  Kata kerja tersebut dipasangkan dengan kata kerja bentuk -ing.

Formula continuous tenses: 'be' + verb-ingFormula present continuous tense: is/am/are + verb-ingFormula past continuous tense: was/were + verb-ingFormula future continuous tense: will be + verb-ing

Berikut contoh kalimat untuk lebih memahami perbedaan ketiga tenses tersebut:

1. I am learning English at the moment. (menjelaskan bahwa aku sedang belajar Bahasa Inggris saat ini, sedang berlangsung dan dimulai sebelum berbicara)

2. I was learning English when Deni called me. (menjelaskan bahwa aku sedang belajar Bahasa Inggris ketika Deni menelpon, dimulai sebelum Deni menelpon)

3. I will be learning English at 7 o'clock this night. (menjelaskan bahwa aku akan sedang belajar jam 7 nanti malam, dimulai sebelum jam 7)

9. perbedaan past continuous tense dengan past perfect continuous tense


Rumus Past Perfect Tense :
(+) S + Had +Verb 3
(-) S + Had +Not +Verb 3
(?) Had + S + Verb 3
Contoh kalimat Past Perfect Tense :
(+) She had just gone (dia baru saja pergi)
(-) she had not just gone
(dia tidak jadi pergi)
(?) had she gone ?
(telahkah dia pergi ?)

Rumus Past Perfect Continuous Tense :
(+) S+had+been+ V3
(-) S+Had+not+been+V3
(?) Had + S + been +V3
Contoh kalimat Past Perfect Continuous Tense :
(+) I had been sleeping
(saya telah tidur)
(-) I had not been sleeping
(saya telah tidak tidur)
(?) Had I been sleeping ?

While & When

Past continuous tense biasanya sering menggunakan kata while dan when.

While digunakan pada klausa past continuous tenseWhen digunakan pada klausa simple past tense

Contoh:

When I arrived, they were talking about me.She went out while I was taking a bath.

Gunakan was untuk subjek I, he, she, itGunakan were untuk you, we, they

27. What are the meanings of present continuous tense, past continuous tense and future continuous tense ?


Jawaban:

1. The present continuous  is a verb tense which is used to show that an ongoing action is happening now, either at the moment of speech or now in a larger sense.

2. The past continuous tense refers to a continuing action or state that was happening at some point in the past.

3. The future continuous tense is a verb tense that indicates that something will occur in the future and continue for an expected length of time.

30. past continuous tense (+),(-),(?)preset continuous tense (+),(-),(?)future continuous tense (+),(-),(?)contoh:​


Jawaban:

Past continuous tense

(+) I was watching a new movie on Netflix when my mother came home last night.

(-) I was not watching a new movie on Netflix when my mother came home last night.

(?) Was I watching a new movie on Netflix when my mother came home  last night?

Preset continuous tense

(+) Our school principal is giving his speech at the moment.

(-) Our school principal is not giving his speech at the moment.

(?) Is our school principal giving his speech at the moment?

Future continuous tense

(+) I will be preparing the report by the time you arrive tomorrow morning.

(-) I will not be preparing the report by the time you arrive tomorrow morning.

(?) Will I be preparing the report by the time you arrive tomorrow morning?

Penjelasan:

Ketiga tenses ini mengandung kata "continuous" (berkelanjutan/terus-menerus) yang mengandung makna bahwa yang dikerjakan itu masih berlangsung.

Dalam Past continuous tense, kondisi masih berlangsung itu dilihat pada suatu titik di masa lampau. (Dalam contoh si atas adalah "when my mother came home last night" (ketika ibuku pulang tadi malam). Jadi kalimat ini bermakna bahwa ketika ibu pulang tadi malam, saya sedang menonton film . Jadi kegiatan menonton itu masih berlangsung ketika ibu pulang, tadi malam (masa lampau)

Dalam Present continuous tense, kondisi masih berlangsung itu dilihat pada suatu titik masa kini atau sekarang (yaitu pada titik waktu ketika sipenutur mengucapkan kata ini).

Contoh kalimat di atas: Our school principal is giving his speech at the moment. (kepala sekolah saat ini sedang berpidato).

Skenarionya misalnya kamu sedang ada di sekolah mendengarkan pidato kepala sekolah, lalu kamu ditelpon mama, lalu kamu bilang "Mom, our school principal is giving his speech at the moment"

Dalam Future continuous tense, kita membayangkan/memperkirakan bahwa di satu  titik waktu di masa depan sesuatu sedang terjadi/dikerjakan.

Dalam contoh di atas: I will be preparing the report by the time you arrive tomorrow moring.
