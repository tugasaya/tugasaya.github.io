16. 1. What does ‘there is’ show ? show the example .2. What does ‘there are’ show ? show the example .bantu plis. . .​


Jawaban:

1. There is a meaning "there"

And some always follow with a single object (single) or the number is only one

Singular nouns followed by the article a or an at the beginning of the word

Example :

a book, a car, a student, a pencil, a bag, a lamp, an umbrella, an apple, an eye, an hour .

sentence example :

There is a glass on tableThere is 1 set of kitchen tool in the kitchenThere is a dress for your party on the bed!There is an apple in refrigeratorThere is an owl on the tree

2. There are means "there"

There are definitely always followed by objects whose number is plural or more than one .

Plural nouns are generally indicated by the ending s or es at the end of the word .

example :

books, students, pencils, cars, boxes, watches dan masih banyak lagi contoh lainnya.

However, please remember ...

that there are also irregular nouns. What this means is that not all objects are plural or more than one in number are marked with an s or es at the end of the word.

example :

children, mice, deer, geese dan lainnya.

sentence example :There are some children in our classroomThere are three pictures on the wallI think there are six cars outsideThere are some boxes in the shopThere are five kinds of flowers in the garden
