20. "Agen perubahan mengadakan lomba poster" → jadiikan ke:1. Simple present tense=(+) =(-) = (?) 2. Simple past tense=(+) =(-) =(?) 3. Simple future tense=(+) =(-) =(?) 4. Present continuos tense=(+) =(-) =(?) 5. Past continuous tense=(+) =(-) =(?) 6. Present perfect tense=(+) =(-) =(?) 7. Past perfect tense=(+) =(-) =(?)​


Jawaban:

1. Simple present tense

(+) Change agents hold a poster contest

=(-) Change agents do not hold a poster contest

= (?) Do change agents hold a poster contest?

2. Simple past tense

=(+) Change agents held a poster contest yesterday

=(-) Change agents did not hold a poster contest yesterday

=(?) Did change agents hold a poster contest yesterday?

3. Simple future tense

=(+) Change agents will hold a poster contest

=(-) Change agents will not hold a poster contest

=(?) Will Change agents hold a poster contest?

4. Present continuos tense

=(+) Change agents are holding a poster contest now

=(-)Change agents are not holding a poster contest now

=(?) Are Change agents holding a poster contest now?

5. Past continuous tense

=(+) Change agents were holding a poster contest

=(-) Change agents were not holding a poster contest

=(?) Were Change agents holding a poster contest?

6. Present perfect tense

=(+) Change agents have held a poster contest

=(-)Change agents have not held a poster contest

=(?) Have Change agents held a poster contest?

7. Past perfect tense

=(+) Change agents had held a poster contest

=(-) Change agents had not held a poster contest

=(?) Had Change agents held a poster contest?
