jari² alas tabung?

RUMUS VOLUME TABUNG

= πr²t

V.tab = πr²t

[tex]4710 = 3,14 \times {r}^{2} \times 15[/tex]

[tex] \frac{4710}{15} = 3,14 \times {r}^{2} [/tex]

[tex]314 = 3,14 \times {r}^{2} [/tex]

[tex] \frac{314}{3,14} = {r}^{2} [/tex]

[tex]100 = {r}^{2} [/tex]

[tex]r = \sqrt{100} [/tex]
