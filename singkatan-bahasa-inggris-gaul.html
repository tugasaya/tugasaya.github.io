19. apa arti PERIOD/PERIODT! dalam bahasa inggris gaul


In slang English or the one for informal situation, the word 'period' means dot or something which signifies the end of the sentence. Another meaning of it is the regular female monthly cycle.

Penjelasan:

The word 'period' has more than one meanings, even for informal situation or slang English. Here are the detail of its meaning as well as the example:

'Period' means dot at the end of the sentence or can be the end of discussion. Example: They must be forgiven, period. I don't want to hear any of your whines about their so-called mistake, while the one who really made one is actually you.another meaning is female regular monthly cycle. Example: Sorry, Vanessa can't make it for today's meeting. She's in pain due to her period.
