Jawaban:

04:15 = It's a quarter past four.05:55 = It's five to six.07:30 = It's half past seven.10:45 = it's a quarter to eleven.08:35 = It's twenty-five to nine.12:40 = It's twenty to one.10:10 = It's ten past ten.05:25 = It's twenty-five past five.06:05 = It's five past six.11:50 = It's ten to twelve.

Penjelasan:

Telling Times

Versi British (Menit + Past/To + Jam)  

09:40 ➔ It's twenty to ten 06:25 ➔ It's twenty-five past six 01:53 ➔ It's seven to two 07:28 ➔ It's twenty-eight past seven 12:15 ➔ It's a quarter past twelve 03:45 ➔ It's a quarter to four 10:30 ➔ It's half past ten

Note:  

Menit 1-29 gunakan (menit + past + jam berada) Menit 31-59 gunakan to (sisa menit + to + jam menuju) Menit ke 15 gunakan a quarter past diikuti jam berada Menit ke 45 gunakan a quarter to diikuti jam menuju Menit ke 30 gunakan half past diikuti jam berada
