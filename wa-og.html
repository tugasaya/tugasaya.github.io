28. What's the definition og analytical exposition​


Jawaban:

Definition of Analytical Exposition. Exposition is a text that elaborates the writer's idea about the phenomenon surrounding. Its social function is to persuade the reader that the idea is important matter.

Penjelasan:

Generic Structure of Analytical Exposition

1. Thesis, introducing the topic and indicating the writer’s position .

2. Arguments, explaining the arguments to support the writer’s position .

3. Reiteration, restating the writer’s position.

The first paragraph usually called thesis, in this part, usually about the writer’s opinion about something. It also introduces topics and indicates the writer’s position.

Then, the second part is argument. It consists of a point and elaboration sequence. Each points must be supported by facts and evidence so as the reader can be influenced with the arguments that the writer stated.

The last part of the generic structure is reiteration. This part is for restates writer’s opinion more fully.
