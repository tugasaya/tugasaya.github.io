40 [tex]dm^{3}[/tex]

Luas permukaan balok = 76 [tex]dm^{2}[/tex]

Volume balok = _____ liter

76 = 2 x [(5 x 2) + (5 x t) + (2 x t)]

76 : 2 = [10 + 5t + 2t]

38 = 10 + 7t

38 – 10 = 7t

28 = 7t

28 : 7 = t

t = 4 cm

Volume balok = p x l x t

= 5 x 2 x 4

= 40 [tex]dm^{3}[/tex]

Jadi volume bingkisan itu adalah 40 [tex]dm^{3}[/tex]
