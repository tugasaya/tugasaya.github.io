27. If he doesn't finish his chores, he.... (will/would)get in trouble


if he doesn't finish his chores, he will get in trouble

Jawaban:

If he doesn't finish his chores, he.... (will/would)get in trouble.

If he doesn't finish his chores, he  will get in trouble.

Penjelasan:

Ini sebuah kalimat pengandaian type satu, yang kalimat utama akan dilakukan atau bisa terjadi manakala syarat terpenuhi. Syarat yaitu kalimat yang ada kata if-nya, kalimnta utama yang ada kat awill-nya, atau modal-nya.

Ru,usnya:

if +subject + verb-1 + object, subject + modal-1 + verb-1 +object.

Aplikasinya:

If (If) +subject (he) + verb-1 (doesn't finish)+ object (his chores), subject (he) + modal-1 (will) + verb-1 (get in) +object (trouble).

Catatan:

modal-1 yaitu will, can, shall, may, must.


28. *Grammar:Present pasive Write completely into passive form 1.My house floor / sweep twice a day My house floor _____ _________ twice a day 2.The cats / feed twice a day _______________________________________ 3.Their mom / help to finish the house chores _______________________________________ 4.The students / teach in the evening after dinner _______________________________________ 5.Some children / train in football match once a week ________________________________________


1. My house floor / sweep twice a day
✐ My house floor is swept twice a day

2.The cats / feed twice a day
✐ the cats are fed twice a day

3.Their mom / help to finish the house chores
✐ their mom is helped to finish the house chores

4.The students / teach in the evening after dinner
✐ the students are taught in the evening after dinner

5.Some children / train in football match once a week
✐ some children are trained in football match once a week

• formula present simple : O + are/am/is + Verb 3 (by S)
