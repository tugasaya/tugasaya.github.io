15. Buatkan 12 kalimat positif, negatif, dan interogatif present perfect tense beserta rumus present perfect tense!​


PRESENT PERFECT TENSE

Rumus untuk kalimat verbal :

(+) S + have / has + verb 3 (past participle)

(-) S + have / has + not + verb 3 (past participle)

(?) Have / has  + S + verb 3 (past participle)

Contoh kalimat verbal :

(+) Father has fixed the broken window

(-) She hasn't recognized the man at first

(?) Have you met her yesterday?

(+) They have completed the task earlier

(-) They haven't built the tree house yet

(?) Has he told you that we have an exam today?

Rumus untuk kalimat nominal :

(+) S + has / have + been + non verb + O

(-) S + has / have + not + been +  non verb + O

(?) Has / have + S + been +  non verb + O

Contoh kalimat nominal :

(+) They have been best friends since they were kid

(-) He hasn't been sick yesterday. So we made an appointment to meet

(?) Has he ever been to abroad before?

(+) There have been many ups and downs in their marriages

(-) It has not been another excellent year for her

(?) Have you been busy for these two weeks? Because you never phoned me

Catatan :

Have digunakan untuk kata ganti orang pertama (I / you / we / they) dan subjek jamak. Has digunakan untuk orang ketiga tunggal (she / he) dan subjek tunggal (it)

20. Apa rumus present perfect tense?


Jawaban:

Rumus Present Perfect Tense

Present perfect tense dibentuk dengan auxiliary verb “have” atau “has”, dan past participle (verb-3). Have digunakan untuk I, you, dan plural subject seperti: plural pronoun (seperti: they, we), plural noun (seperti: boys, men), dan compound subject dengan kata hubung “and” (seperti: you and I, Tom and Jack); sedangkan has untuk singular subject seperti: third person singular pronoun (seperti: he, she, it) dan singular noun (seperti: Tom, man).

Past participle dibentuk dengan menambahkan -ed, -en, -d, -t, -n, atau -ne pada base form berupa regular verb. Pada base form berupa irregular verb, bentuk past participle tidak konsisten.

Lihat:

Daftar Irregular Verb

rumus present perfect tense: has/have + past participle *Has digunakan untuk singular subject, kecuali: I dan you; have untuk I, you, dan plural subject.

rumus present perfect tense

Berikut rumus present perfect tense untuk kalimat positif, negatif, dan interogatif.

Rumus Present Perfect Tense Contoh Present Perfect Tense

kalimat positif (+)

S + auxiliary verb (have/has) + past participle (verb-3)

I have read the book

He has left

kalimat negatif (-)

S + auxiliary verb (have/has) + not + past participle (verb-3)

I have not read the book

He hasn’t left

kalimat interogatif (?)

auxiliary verb (have/has) + S + past participle (verb-3)

Have I read the book

Has he left

23. Rumus present perfect continous tense?


Rumus Present Perfect Continuous Tense adalah(+) S + has/have + been + V-ing + O/C(-) S + has/have + not + been + V-ing + O/C(?) has/have + S + been + V-ing + O/C?

PEMBAHASAN Present Perfect Continuous

Digunakan untuk menyatakan kegiatan yang sudah selesai di masa lalu atau kegiatan yang sudah dimulai di masa lalu dan terus berlanjut hingga sekarang.

Contoh Kalimatnya(+) I have been waiting the teacher in the class for an hour.(-) I have not been waiting the teacher in the class for an hour.(?) have I been waiting the teacher in the class for an hour?

Catatan:

➡ Has untuk subject He/She/it/Satu nama

➡ Have untuk subject I/You/We/They/Dua nama

I have [disingkat menjadi] I’veI have been => I’ve been

Jadi, keduanya [I have atau I’ve] sama saja.

28. Sebutkan Rumus(Patterns) dari:-Simple Past Tense-Present Continuous Tense-Present Perfect Tense​


Jawaban:

Simple past tense

Positive (+)

· Subject + verb 2 + object · Subject + to be (was/were) + adjective/adverb

Negative (-)

· Subject + did not + infinitive verb + object · Subject + was/were + not + adjective/adverb

Interrogative (?)

· Did + subject + infinitive verb + object?

· Was/were + subject + adjective/adverb?

Present continues tense

Positive (+)

Subject + be (am/is/are) + verb (-ing) + …

Negative(-)

Subjek +be (am/is/are) + not + verb (-ing) + …

Bentuk interrogative (?)

Be (am/is/are) + subjek + verb (-ing) + …?

Present perfect tense

Positive (+)

(+) · I/You/We/They + have + verb 3

· He/She/It + has + verb 3

Negative (-)

(-) · I/You/We/They + have + not + verb 3

· He/She/It + has + not + verb 3

Interrogative (?)

(?) · Have + I/You/We/They + verb 3?

· Has + He/She/It + verb 3?

30. rumus active dari present perfect continous, past future perfect continous tense, present future perfect continous tense beserta contohnya


Present Perfect Continous
(+) S + Have/Has + Been + V-ing + O/C >>> She has been eating the cake for 2 hours
(-) S + Have/Has + not + Been + V-ing + O/C >>> She hasn't been eating the cake for 2 hours
(?) Have/Has + S + Been + V-ing + O/C? >>> Has she been eating the cake for 2 hours?

Past Future Perfect Continous
(+) S + Would + Have + Been + V-ing + O/C >>> She would have been eating cake for two hours
(-) S + Would + not + Have + Been + V-ing + O/C >>> She wouldn't have been eating the cake for 2 hours
(?) Would + S + Have + Been + V-ing + O/C? >>> Would she have been eating the cake for 2 hours?

Future Perfect Continous
(+) S + Will + Have + Been + V-ing + O/C >>> She will have been eating cake for two hours
(-) S + Will + not + Have + Been + V-ing + O/C >>> She won't have been eating the cake for 2 hours
(?) Will + S + Have + Been + V-ing + O/C? >>> Will she have been eating the cake for 2 hours?
