20. buatlah kata kata simple past tense ( (+), (-),(?) )​


Past simple

We use past simple for actions or events that happened one after the another( kejadian di masa lampau) We use verb 2 for past simple ex: bought,could,did,gave,etc

(+) I ate pizza yesterday ( saya makan pizza kemarin)

(-) I didnt go to the cinema last night( saya tidak pergi ke bioskop tadi malam). If we want to use negative form we use didnt+base form (V1)

(?) Did you visit your grandma last week? ( Apakah kamu berkunjung ke rumah nenekmu minggu lalu?)

Simple Past Tense

Buatlah kalimat simple past tense (+)(-)(?)

Jawaban

Berikut ini adalah contoh kalimat 'simple past tense'.

1.

(+) I did my homework yesterday.

(-) I didn't do my homework yesterday.

(?) Did I do my homework yesterday?

= Yes, you did. atau No, you didn't

2.

(+) Anna and I went to a grocery store two days ago.

(-) Anna and I didn't go to a grocery store two days ago.

(?) Did Anna and I go to a grocery store two days ago?

= Yes, you did. atau No, you didn't.

3.

(+) Edward and Jimmy went on a vacation.

(-) Edward and Jimmy didn't go on a vacation.

(?) Did Edward and Jimmy go on a vacation?

= Yes, they did. atau No, they didn't.

4.

(+) I was in the garage last night.

(-) I wasn't in the garage last night.

(?) Was I in the garage last night?

= Yes, you were. atau No, you weren't

5.

(+) You were an English teacher.

(-) You weren't an English teacher.

(?) Were you an English teacher?

= Yes, I was. atau No, I wasn't.

___________

Penjelasan

Rumus yang digunakan untuk membuat kalimat di jawaban adalah sebagai berikut.

[Verbal Sentence]

(+) S + V2 + O

(-) S + didn't + V1 + O

(?) Did + S + V1 + O?

[Nominal Sentence]

(+) S + was/were + C

(-) S + wasn't/weren't + C

(?) Was/Were + S + C?

*S = Subject; V = Verb; O = Object; C = Complement
