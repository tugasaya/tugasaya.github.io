5. resep makanan nasi goreng​


Jawaban:

Nasi goreng rumahan

Bahan:

- Minyak

- Daun bawang 1 batang. Iris halus

- Cabai merah 3 buah

- Daging ayam 125 g. Cincang halus

- Telur 1 butir. Kocok

- Kecap manis 2 sdm

- Bawang merah 5 siung

- Garam 1 sdt

- Merica sdt

- Nasi 600 gr

- Bawang putih 3 siung

Cara memasak:

a. Masukkan bawang putih, merah, dan cabai merah ke dalam cobek kemudian haluskan.

b. Goreng telur menjadi orak-arik, sisihkan.

c. Tumis bumbu yang sudah dihaluskan dengan minyak secukupnya hingga harum.

d. Masukkan ayam cincang, telur dan daun bawang ke dalam bumbu. Tumis lagi hingga rata. Tambahkan kecap, garam, dan merica. Aduk hingga rata.

e. Kemudian masukkan nasi dan aduk hingga rata.

13. Resep nasi goreng dalam bahasa inggris dan artinya


Jawaban:

Material:

- 2 eggs

- 2 tablespoons of cooking oil

- 3 cherry tomatoes (cut to taste)

- 1 plate of rice

- 1 scallion (roughly chopped)

- Crackers (to taste)

- 1 tablespoon margarine

Ground spices:

- 2 cloves of garlic

- 5 pieces of cayenne pepper

- Sweet soy sauce (to taste)

- 3 cloves of shallot

- Salt to taste)

- Sugar (to taste)

How to cook:

a. Heat margarine until melted, cook scrambled eggs and set aside.

b. Add cooking oil and spices, stir well, cook until fragrant.

c. Enter rice into the egg mixture and seasoning, stir well. Add leeks, stir again.

d. Serve with cherry tomato topping and crackers as well as a little celery leaf garnish and red chili slices.

Artinya ;

Bahan:

- 2 telur

- 2 sendok makan minyak goreng

- 3 tomat ceri (potong secukupnya)

- 1 piring nasi

- 1 daun bawang (cincang kasar)

- Kerupuk (sesuai selera)

- 1 sendok makan margarin

Bumbu halus:

- 2 siung bawang putih

- 5 potong cabai rawit

- Kecap manis (secukupnya)

- 3 siung bawang merah

- Garam secukupnya)

- Gula untuk dicicip)

Cara memasak:

Sebuah. Panaskan margarin sampai meleleh, masak telur dadar dan sisihkan.

b. Tambahkan minyak goreng dan rempah-rempah, aduk rata, masak sampai harum.

c. Masukkan nasi ke dalam campuran telur dan bumbu, aduk rata. Tambahkan daun bawang, aduk lagi.

d. Sajikan dengan taburan tomat ceri dan kerupuk serta sedikit hiasan daun seledri dan irisan cabai merah.

seafood fried rice recipeSea Food Fried Rice (Nasi Goreng Sea Food)
MATERIALS
500 gr of cold white rice2 eggs6 pieces of small shrimp that has been peeled1 piece of squid that ink, cuticle, and bone have been discarded, and cut roundly.1 leek that has been cut roundly1 packet of Complete Seasoning Fried Rice2 tablespoons of cooking oil1 teaspoon of margarine
HOW TO COOK
Heat the oil that has been mixed with margarine and enter the shrimp and squid. Cook until the color change then add the egg and green onion, and cook again until done.
After the dishes are already quite mature, add white rice and fried rice seasoning, stir until going well mixed and take the fried rice.

Once fried rice cooked, you can serve it on a serving plate and added with other complementary ingredients to taste like chili sauce, pickles, crackers, fried onions, and others.



Crispy Fried Rice (Nasi Goreng Crispy)
MATERIALS
400 grams of white rice2 eggs1 pc of chicken thigh bones removed and cut into pieces of lighters3 tablespoons all-purpose flour seasoning2 tablespoons cooking oil1 teaspoon of margarine1 Complete Fried Rice SeasoningEnough Salt and pepper
HOW TO COOK
Heat oil and margarine in a cooking pan. After it starts to heat up, then enter the scrambled eggs until eggs are cooked and add fried rice seasoning into the cooking pan. Stir until all ingredients mixed well.
Once the rice is mixed well and set aside on a serving plate first.
The way in order to process the chicken pieces are kneaded with salt and pepper. Then, the chicken dipped in white egg and wrapped with all-purpose flour with a pressed one by one. It is done so that one and the other are not sticky.
Heat the oil and fry the chicken until turns to be golden browned.Once chicken is cooked, sprinkle over fried rice that had been prepared the plate before serving, voila crispy fried rice is ready to eat.


Mango Fried Rice (Nasi Goreng Mangga)
MATERIALS
500 grams of white rice3 point veal meatballs, chicken, or fish which are cut into small dice1 young mango cut into small dice2 scrambled eggs are thin and cut into pieces, do not forget to add salt and pepper½ carrot cut into small dice2 tablespoons cooking oil1 teaspoon of margarine1 packet of Complete Seasoning Fried Rice
HOW TO COOK
Heat oil and margarine together and then add the carrots. Cook until carrots turns to be cooked then add the meatballs. Add the mango, white rice, fried rice seasoning. Stir constantly until the ingredients blend well and then turn off the flame.

Serve mango fried rice on a serving dish and sprinkle with pieces of fried egg on it. Mango fried rice was ready to be served.

Vegetables Fried Rice (Nasi Goreng Sayuran)
MATERIALS

HOW TO COOK
Heat the cooking oil and mix with margarine. Add carrot, beans, spring onions and stir-fry until cooked. Then, enter the sweet corn and tomatoes. Saute until the vegetables are slightly wilted. After that, add the egg, cook until done and add seasoning fried rice. Stir well to blend the seasonings.
Vegetable fried rice is ready. Serve with a funny shape so that your child will eat more spirit. You can also add other complementary materials such as crackers, fried onions, or pickles. Let’s live a healthy life.

18. Resep Bahan Nasi Goreng Tetelan Sapi !​


GAMBAR NASI GORENG TETELAN SAPI

Resep :

1. Haluskan bumbu kemudian tumis hingga harum.

2. Masukan tetelan sapi dan nasi, aduk hingga nasi tdk saling menempel.

3. Kemudian masukan kecap, aduk lagi hingga sedikit kering. Angkat sajikan

Bahan :

1 piring nasi

secukupnya Tetelan sapi

5 buah cabe

1 siung bawang putih

2 buah bawang merah

1/2 biji kemiri

Garam, penyedap

Kecap

Penjelasan :

cara cara :

Sambal goreng tetelan sapi & udang goreng tepung menu rumahan simpel sederhana. Sekarang, nasi goreng gak sekedar nasi tumis berbumbu yang diberi telur dadar atau mata sapi saja. Ada banyak jenis nasi goreng yang juga gak kalah Nasi goreng ini, konon katanya berasal dari kota Semarang, Jawa Tengah.

Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang digunakan dalam pembuatan Nasi Goreng Tetelan Sapi:

Sediakan 1 piring nasi.

Siapkan secukupnya Tetelan sapi.

Gunakan 5 buah cabe.

Siapkan 1 siung bawang putih.

Gunakan 2 buah bawang merah.

Gunakan 1/2 biji kemiri.

Ambil Garam, penyedap.

Sediakan Kecap.

Seperti nasi goreng khas Jawa pada umumnya, nasi goreng babat juga. Kuliner nasi goreng hampir setiap saat bisa tersaji. Tentu setiap orang mengenal makanan khas asli Indonesia ini. Pamor nasi goreng sebagai menu kuliner Indonesia yang memiliki cita rasa enak telah dikenal masyarakat luas sejak dulu hingga kini.

Cara membuat Nasi Goreng Tetelan Sapi:

Haluskan bumbu kemudian tumis hingga harum.

Masukan tetelan sapi dan nasi, aduk hingga nasi tdk saling menempel..

Kemudian masukan kecap, aduk lagi hingga sedikit kering. Angkat sajikan.

Dari dalam negeri sampai ke luar negeri. Masukkan minyak, goreng telur orak-arik, sisihkan. Kemudian tumis cabai rawit dan daun bawang yang sudah diiris.

20. Permutasi dari kata:-Nasi-Goreng-Enak​

[tex]\huge\bf\color{66B2FF}{✎Pembahasan}[/tex]

Pengertian Permutasi

Permutasi adalah banyaknya susunan yang dapat dibentuk dari suatu kumpulan objek baik sebagian atau seluruhnya dengan urutan penyusunan yang berbeda dari sebelumnya.

Rumus Permutasi

Jika terdapat unsur ganda

[tex]\boxed{\tt{P \: = \frac{n!}{k!} }}[/tex]

Jika tidak ada unsur ganda

[tex]\boxed{\tt{P = n!}}[/tex] Keterangan

P adalah permutasi

n adalah total unsur

k adalah unsur ganda

! adalah faktorial

[tex]\huge\color{00CCCC}\tt{✏PENYELESAIAN}[/tex]

• NasiTotal unsur = 4Unsur ganda = -

P = n!

= 4!

= 4×3×2×1

= 24 susunan

[tex] \\ [/tex]

• GorengTotal unsur = 6Unsur ganda = 2(G)

P = n!/k!

= 6!/2!

= 6×5×4×3×2×1 / 2×1

= 720/2

= 360 susunan

[tex] \\ [/tex]

• EnakTotal unsur = 4Unsur ganda = -

P = n!

= 4!

= 4×3×2×1

= 24 susunan

Ingredients:

2 cloves of Garlic
2 onions cut into small pieces
1 table spoon of vegetable oil a plate of rice

.First, pound garlic and 2 pinches of salt.

.Second, heat vegetable oil on a frying pan.

.Third, put the pounded garlic and salt, and onions into the hot vegetable oil. Fry them a while.

.Fourth, put one plate of rice. Fry and mix them around 3 menit.

.Fifth, add two pinches of salt. Continue frying until the rice is hot enough.
