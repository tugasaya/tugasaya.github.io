1. Pengertian obligation. Struktur obligation dan Contoh kalimat obligation?


Expressing Obligations

Expressing Obligation is an expression in English which is used to express a necessity or something that you have to do as an obligation.

The structure:

Subject + must + verb 1 + object Subject + must + be + Adjective/noun/adverbSubject + have to/has to + verb 1 + object Subject + have to/has to + be +  Adjective/noun/adverb

The examples:

All students must come to school at 7 a.m. He really must study harder it if he wants to pass the examination     We must obey school rules without exception.  I have to go to school at seven o’clock. We have to show ID cards.

PEMBAHASAN:

Expressing Obligation adalah sebuah ekspresi dalam Bahasa Inggris yang digunakan untuk menyatakan sebuah keharusan atau sesuatu yang harus kamu lakukan sebagai suatu kewajiban.

Ketika kita mengungkapkan keharusan dalam bahasa Inggris kita bisa menggunakan kata must dan have to. Kata “must” dipergunakan untuk menyatakan sesuatu yang harus dilakukan biasanya berhubungan dengan aturan. Have to merupakan modal verb yang dipergunakan untuk menunjukan apa yang harus kita lakukan.

23. please write some sentences showing obligation, prohibition , advice obligation - - - prohibition - - - advice / suggestion - - -


Jawaban:

Suggestion/Recommendation

The social function of Suggestion is to express what are should we/they/I do.

For examples :

• You should change your attitude.

• You should love your body.

• We should ask Mrs. Ariyani to open the seminar.

Keyword in suggestion is should

Respond :

• Okay

• Alright

• Of course

• Certainly

• Sure

Structure

1. S + should + V1 + (O) + (adv)

  You should study hard.

Example dialog :

Miss Sofi : "You'd better put your items into a backpack and handbag. You should love your body."

Edo : "You could be right. Thanks for your suggestion, Miss.

Miss Sofi : "My pleasure"

Obligation

The sosial function of obligation is to express what are must we/they/I do.

For examples :

• We must wear a uniform every day.

• You have to study hard.

• You has to do five prayers on time.

Keywords in obligation is must and has/have to

Structure

1. S + must + V1 + (O) + (adv)

  We must wear a uniform every day.

2. S + have to + V1 + (O) + (adv)

  You have to do five prayers on time.

Example paragraph :

 We must wear a uniform every day. From Monday to Thursday we must wear the batik shirt. The girls must wear a black skirt, and boys must wear a pair of black pants. On Friday we must wear the Scout uniform. We must wear proper shoes.

Prohibition

The social function of prohibition is to express what are must not we/they/I do.

For examples :

• We must not be late to school.

• We must not litter.

• You don't have to play truant the class.

• You don't has to skip the lesson.

• You don't break the rules

Keywords in prohibition is must not, don't have/don't has, and don't

Respond :

• Yes

• Ok

• Certainly

Structure

1. S + must not/musn't + V1 + ( O ) + (adv)

  We must not be late to school.

2. S + don't have/has to + V1 + ( O ) + (adv)

  You don't have to play truant the lesson.

3. S + don't + V1 +  (O) + (adv)

  You don't break the rules.

Example dialog :

Beni : "Udin, let's go out. Don't be lazy. Get up and have some exercise outside"

Udin : "Okay. Wait. Let me put on my shoes."
