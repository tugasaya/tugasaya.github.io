8. A scout meeting lasts 2 hours. If 36 minutes of the meeting is spent making dream catchers, what percentage of the meeting is left?


2 hours = 2 × 60 minutes = 120 minutes

120 minutes - 36 minutes = 84minutes

84/120 × 100% = 70%

Answer : 70%
