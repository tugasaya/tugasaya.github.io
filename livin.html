12. Tentukan celsius, reamur, dan kelivin dari 59 derajat fahrenheit = dengan caranya


Dik :
T°F = 59°F

Dit :
a) T°C =....°C ?
b) T°R =....°R
c) TK =.....K

Jawab :
a) T°C = 5 / 9 (T°F - 32)
          = 5 / 9 (59 - 32) = 15°C

b) T°R = 4 / 9 (T°F - 32)
          = 4 / 9 (59 - 32) = 12°R

c) TK = T°C +273 = 15 + 273 = 288 K

Jawaban:

Facts:

1 "If she had come early, I would have seen her.

Facts: She did not come early and I did not see her.

2 I would have been okay, If my friend would have called me

Facts: My friend did not call me and I was not okay.

B. Complete the following sentences !

1. If my mother had given me a present, I would have been even more active in learning

2 He wouldn't have been sad if she had visited him.

3. If Sinta had called me, I would have felt Happy.

C. Make Conditional based on the situation below!

1. I was happy because my father gave me good news.

If my Father had not given me good news, I would have been sad.

2. My Car was good, so my family went to Bandung​

If my car had been in a bad condition, my family would have stayed at home.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Conditional Sentences (kalimat pengandaian / kalimat bersyarat) tipe 3 dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Fakta-fakta:

1 "Jika dia (cewek) datang lebih awal, aku akan melihatnya.

Fakta: Dia (cewek) tidak datang lebih awal dan aku tidak melihatnya.

2 Aku akan baik-baik saja, Jika temanku meneleponku

Fakta: Temanku tidak meneleponku dan aku tidak baik-baik saja.

B. Lengkapi kalimat berikut!

1. Jika ibuku memberiku hadiah, aku akan lebih aktif dalam belajar

2 Dia (cowok) tidak akan sedih jika dia (cewek) mengunjunginya.

3. Jika Sinta meneleponku, aku akan merasa Bahagia.

C. Buatlah Conditional berdasarkan situasi di bawah ini!

1. Aku senang karena ayahku memberiku kabar baik.

Jika Ayahku tidak memberiku kabar baik, aku akan sedih.

2. Mobilku (dalam kondisi) bagus, jadi keluargaku pergi ke Bandung​

Jika mobil saya dalam kondisi buruk, keluargaku akan tinggal di rumah.

Kalimat pengandaian tipe 3:

If+ subject + had + V3/past perfect Tense + subject + would have + V3/past perfect Tense

atau

if+ subject + had been + adjective/noun + subject + would have been + adjective/noun
