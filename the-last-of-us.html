Simple Past Tense

JAWABAN:

I. Change the verbs in the brackets into the correct form of Simple Past Tense!

1. (+) We worked in our garden yesterday.

  (-) We did not work in our garden yesterday

  (?) Did we work in our garden yesterday?

2. (+) I made a cake for my mother’s birthday two days ago.

    (-) I did not make a cake for my mother’s birthday two days ago.

    (?) Did I make a cake for my mother’s birthday two days ago?

3. (+) Yoshiko and I talked by telephone yesterday.

    (-) Yoshiko and I did not talk by telephone yesterday.

    (?) Did Yoshiko and I talk by telephone yesterday?

4.  (?) Did the meeting last about two hours?

   (+) The meeting lasted about two hours

   (-) The meeting did not last about two hours

5. (+) Mrs. Jason visited her son a month ago.

   (-) Mrs. Jason did not visit her son a month ago.

   (?) Did Mrs. Jason visit her son a month ago?

6. (+) We both liked the movie last night.        

    (-) We both did not like the movie last night.

    (?) Did we both like the movie last night?

7.  (?) Did you sleep very well last night?

   (+) You slept very well last night    

   (-) You did not sleep very well last night

8. (+) They swam in the lake last week.

   (-) They did not swim in the lake last week

   (?) Did they swim in the lake last week?

9. (+) Ms. Burns came to visit us last night.

    (-) Ms. Burns did not come to visit us last night.

    (?) Did Ms. Burns come to visit us last night?

10. (?) Did they tell us about their plans for the new home?

    (+) They told us about their plans for the new home

    (-) They did not tell us about their plans for the new home

PEMBAHASAN:

Simple past tense digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian di waktu tertentu di masa lampau.

Time expression:  

yesterday (kemarin) last night/week/month/year/time (semalam / minggu lalu / tahun lalu / waktu yang lalu) this morning (pagi ini) one hour/day/week/month/year ago (satu jam/hari/minggu/bulan/ tahun lalu) in February (di bulan Februari) in 2020 (pada tahun 2020) on Friday (pada hari Jumat) when + S + verb-2 (when he came, when I was a child)

Formula:

Verbal sentences:

(+)  Subject + Verb2 (-)   Subject + did + not + Verb1  (?)  Did + Subject + Verb1?

Nominal sentence:

(+)  Subject + to be (was/were) + O/C (-)   Subject + to be (was/ were) + O/C  (?)  To be (was/ were) +Subject + O/C?

Perhatikan penjelasan bagaimana menggunakan to be di dalam kalimat nominal berikut ini!

Tobe "was" digunakan untuk subject I, he, she dan it atau kata nama diri dalam bentuk tunggal. Tobe "were" digunakan untuk subject you, we dan they atau kata nama diri dalam bentuk jamak.

Writing sentence in simple past tense:


1. (+) John gave the magazine to me yesterday

(-) John did not give the magazine to me yesterday


2. (+) Mr. Refi told us the legend of Lumajang last week

(?) Did Mr. Refi tell us the legend of Lumajang last week?


Penjelasan

Simple past tense adalah kalimat yang menunjukan keterangan waktu di masa lalu. Berikut adalah formulanya:


(+) Subject + V2

I watched TV last weekHe met his wife 10 years ago

(-) Subject + did NOT + V1

She didn’t finish the exercise yesterdayI didn’t go to school last week

(?) Did + Subject + V1?

Did you see me yesterday?Did Bella take her medicine?
