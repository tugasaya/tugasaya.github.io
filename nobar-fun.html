Jawaban:

a. I think mathematics is fun

Penjelasan:

In this sentence, "I" is the subject, "think" is the verb, "mathematics" is the object, and "fun" is the adjective. We use "is" as to be or auxiliary verb. Thus "I think mathematics is fun." is the correct sentence.
